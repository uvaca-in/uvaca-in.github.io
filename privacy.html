Privacy

We are committed to protecting your personal information and respecting your privacy

By using our website, you consent to Uvaca' collection and use of information in accordance with the terms of this Privacy Policy. We are committed to protecting your personal information and respecting your privacy. Except as set forth in this Privacy Policy, we will not use, disclose, sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third parties, unless you give us your permission or we are legally compelled to do so, such as by law, subpoena, or court order, or unless such disclosure or use becomes necessary for the protection of our legal interests or to prevent harm to Uvaca or third parties.

Information collection

The kind of information we collect from you online depends on how you decide to use our website or services. If you choose simply to browse our site, we will collect the information from your computing device that is necessary to enable us to allow you to browse our website, but you will not need to submit any additional personal information at all. However, you might choose to provide us with your personally identifiable information by such means as e-mailing us, sending us your resume, or filling out certain forms on our website. When you do so, you understand and agree that we will use the personally identifiable information you provide to us to provide you with our services and products. Our system may also gather certain technical data about your visit, which helps us understand better how our site is being used and how we can improve it.

Information use

E-mail Contact:  If you choose to contact us by e-mail, we will use reasonable efforts to keep your contact information private although we may use it to respond to your message. No confidentiality will apply to the subject matter of your message, however, so we recommend that you do not send us any ideas or suggestions that you consider proprietary or confidential. By providing us e-mail, you understand and agree that you are expressly providing Uvaca a license to use and fully exploit the information in your email (except your contact information). Solicited Information: We may ask website visitors for personal information through forms on our website, including, for example, registering for events, joining our mailing list, becoming a business partner, or inquiring about product sales. The personal information you choose to provide on these forms, which would typically include your name, company name, and contact information, may be used by Uvaca in a manner consistent with the stated purpose of the given form. In addition, we may use visitor responses for non-personal assessment of their preferences so we can tailor our site's contents to better suit the tastes and needs of our audience. If you are asked for your e-mail address in a form on our website, we will not add it to any automatic mailing lists unless we've asked for permission to do so. Employment Opportunities: Our site may provide you with a means for submitting your resume or other personal information for consideration for employment opportunities at Uvaca. Personal information received through resume submissions will be kept confidential in accordance with Uvaca' standard HR policies. We may contact you for additional information to supplement your resume, and we may use your information within Uavca, or keep it on file for future use, as we make our hiring decisions. Automatically Gathered Data: We may store and use information gathered automatically by our system--such as computer IP addresses, the kind of browsers our viewers are using, and a record of the pages viewed on our site--so we can understand how to make our site more convenient and useful to our audience. We may also share this information, in aggregate form, with parties we hire to help us perform our business. Transfer of Data: In order to promote continuity of services and support, if Uvaca' business is sold, acquired, or merges with another company, we may transfer the information you've shared with us to the successor company. If this occurs, the use and disclosure of transferred data will be governed by the privacy practices of the successor company.

Managing your privacy

You may request deletion or modification of your other personal information by contacting us at contact-us@uvaca.com. Note that any deleted information may remain in our data store as a result of routine system back-ups, but we will no longer use that information.

Outside links

Our website may contain links to other sites. Please be aware that these third-party sites may collect information about you, and that they operate according to their own privacy practices. If you have any questions about linking to our site, contact contact-us@uvaca.com.

Children's privacy protection

Uvaca, its website, and its products are not directed to children. We will not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under 13. If you believe we have collected personally identifiable information from a minor under the age of 13 for whom you are responsible, please provide us with the relevant information by emailing us at contact-us@uvaca.com.

Questions?
